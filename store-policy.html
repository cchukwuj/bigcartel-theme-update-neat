Return and Exchange Policy 

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are happy to process an exchange of your purchase for eligible items. Unfortunately, refunds are not available at this time.

Exchanges must be requested within one week of delivery. Items must be in their original condition without any signs of wear or damage. Customers are responsible for paying return and exchange shipping fees.

To initiate an exchange, please email lgndryapparelco@gmail.com with your order number and the items you wish to return.
